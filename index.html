About Us

A Confluence of Culture, Heritage & Innovation

The Bidar Cultural Summit 2026 brings together thought leaders, artists, historians, and innovators for three days of immersive experiences celebrating the rich tapestry of Karnataka's cultural heritage.

From keynote sessions with renowned speakers to interactive workshops, art installations, and cultural performances, this summit promises to be a transformative journey through time and tradition.
