Established in 2020, Heritage Foundation India is a leading non-profit organization dedicated to preserving and promoting India's rich cultural heritage. With over 100+ heritage projects across the country, we've touched millions of lives and revived dozens of dying art forms.

Our work spans heritage conservation, artisan empowerment, cultural education, and digital archiving. We believe that heritage is not just about the past‚Äîit's about creating a sustainable future that honors our roots while embracing innovation.
