Where History Comes Alive

Nestled in the heart of Karnataka, Bidar is a treasure trove of architectural brilliance and cultural heritage. Once the capital of the mighty Bahmani Sultanate, this historic city stands as a testament to centuries of artistic excellence, religious harmony, and architectural innovation. From majestic forts to serene gurudwaras, from ancient madrasas to mystical springs, Bidar invites you to step back in time and experience the grandeur of medieval India.

Karez Water System

Ingenious Underground Water Network

The Karez Water System of Bidar is a remarkable example of medieval engineering and sustainable water management. Introduced during the Bahmani rule in the 15th century, this underground irrigation system was designed to supply clean drinking water to the city without evaporation loss.
